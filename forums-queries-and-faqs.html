Frequently asked Questions

Do you ship game products here as well as overseas?

We ship games as well as game consoles and peripherals islandwide. We also ship overseas, but it comes with a shipping cost.

How long does a single delivery take?

A single delivery can take from 2 to 5 days, depending on the speed of the delivery and the distance needed to travel to deliver the specified products.

What payment methods are accepted?

Credit/debit cards, PayPal, gift cards, and sometimes cryptocurrency.

Are there refunds for purchases?

Refund policies vary. Check the platform's terms and conditions.
